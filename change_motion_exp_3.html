Object Experiment

Welcome! Thank you for participating in our study.

Important, before you begin:

Please do this experiment on a laptop or desktop computer. If you are viewing this experiment on a phone or a tablet, please close the window and return later with non-mobile device.

Second: please maximize the window for this browser and tab before you continue.

Click 'continue' once you have maximized the window and you are ready to move on.

In this experiment every trial will be more or less the same: 2 colored disks will be presented to the left and right of a central occluder. Novel shapes will appeare in the two disks. The shapes then will be removed, and the empty colored disks will move behind the occluder. The occluder will then be removed to display the two colored disks containing two shapes. If both shapes are the same as in the preview, press 1 to indicate they are the same. If one is replaced by a new shape, press 0 to indicate there is a new shape. The positions of the shapes and the colors of the disks in which they appear are entirely irrelevant to the task, so you should base your decision solely on the shapes themselves.

Click 'start training' to see the training trials.

Training is now complete. The experiment is about to start. The experiemnt will take around 40 mintues.

Click 'start experiment' when you are ready. Remember, your job is to press 1 or 0 on the keyboard as fast as possible to indicate whether the two shapes are the same as you saw before the disks move, or there is a new shape.

click 'next' to continue.

The experiment is finished. Please click 'submit' to submit your data. Thank you.
